About Me

I was born and raised in the Kansas City, Kansas where I was educated. I graduated
from high school in the top 10% of my class and earned several academic awards. I
began my undergraduate studies at Kansas City, Kansas Community College on a
Presidential Honors Scholarship. and graduated. I continued my studies in Lawrence,
Kansas at The University of Kansas where I studied Accounting. After being faced
with the end to a 10 year career in manufacturing, I returned to college and it was at
the University of Phoenix-Kansas City where I completed my degree in Business
Management in 2008. Recently, I decided to switch careers and took on a new endea-
vor to become a Software Engineer through Momentum@Morehouse

When I am not working, you may find me in a kitchen cooking for friends and/or family.
(The kitchen is my happy place!) I also enjoy reading, doing puzzles, and traveling
